Let's Learn About Child Abuse

What is CHILD ABUSE?

Child abuse is when someone harms a child or young person. This harm can be physical, emotional, or sexual. It's important to understand what abuse is so we can recognize it and get help if we need it.

Types of Abuse

  • Physical Abuse
  • Emotional Abuse
  • Sexual Abuse
  • Neglect

How to Get Help

If you or someone you know is experiencing abuse, it's important to tell a trusted adult, like a parent, teacher, or counselor. You can also call a helpline for support.
